My first exposure to computing was when my dad bought a Windows 95 computer. I still remember when my cousin came to visit and created a Lycos email and our elementary school's favorite game was Oregon Trail.

Ah, the nostalgia.

My first exposure to programming language was when I was in sixth grade learning Visual Basic. Over the years, I was taught C and C++ in High School and Python in College. After I finished college, I self-taught myself Ruby and Javascript. I joined codepen to learn more about HTML/CSS/JS and was blown away by what CSS/JS can do together.

My current technology stack is Ruby on Rails and React. I use React-Native for mobile developments. I am experienced with Angular, Ember, and vanilla JS as well. Check out my codepen or projects page to see what I have done.

If you have a small project in mind, get in touch and maybe your next coffee is on me!
