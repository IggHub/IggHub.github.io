My first exposure to computing was when my dad bought a Windows 95 computer. I still remember when my cousin came to visit and created a Lycos email (using dial-up connection. I would seldom fought with my dad who needed to use the phone for work).

My first programming language was Visual Basic, which I learned in 6th grade when my mom would take me to extracurricular CS course. Over the years, I was taught C and C++ in High School and Python in College. After I finished college, I self-taught myself Ruby and Javascript. I joined codepen to learn more about HTML/CSS/JS and was blown away by what CSS/JS can do together.

My current technology stack is Ruby on Rails and React. I use React-Native for mobile developments. I have experienced developing with Angular1, Ember, and vanilla JS as well.
